The 21 Best Off-Grid Communities in the World, There's One for You

Written by: UGREEN

|

September 15, 2023

|

Time to read 17 min

Imagine a life where the constant buzz of electricity fades away, and you can hear the gentle rustling of leaves and the soft bubbling of a stream nearby. That's what off-grid communities are all about! These are places where more and more people are choosing to live in a new way. They're saying goodbye to usual things like electricity from big power companies and saying hello to a life that's more connected to nature. 

These special off-grid communities are on the mission of taking care of themselves and Mother Earth on their own. In this article, we're going to explore the 22 best off-grid communities around the world. It's like going on an adventure to find amazing places where people have chosen to live differently, away from the usual way of life. Let’s give off-grid communities a thought!

Here we have compiled and presented a list of the best off-grid communities for you to enjoy a luxurious and heavenly experience while living off the grid communities. 

Best Off-Grid Comminities #1 Greater World Earthship Community

Picture this: a hidden gem in the heart of Taos, New Mexico, United States where the Greater World Earthship Community thrives. It's like stepping into a world where houses are more than just buildings—they're living, breathing parts of nature. These incredible off-grid communities, called Earthships, are crafted from recycled stuff like old tires, glass bottles, and cans, blending the goodness of nature with smart design.

The Greater World Earthship Community isn't just a place to live; it's a whole way of life that dances with nature's beat. Each off-grid community is super smart. They use sunlight to power up, gather rainwater for all sorts of things, and even treat wastewater the natural way. It's a totally cool way of living off-grid communities that lots of nature-loving folks are falling in love with! 

If you're all about connecting with nature and waving goodbye to the usual modern life, the Greater World Earthship Community might just be your dream come true.

Best Off-Grid Comminities #2 Earthaven 

A heavenly location tucked away in the lush forests of North Carolina, United States. That's Earthaven for you—a special place where local travelers are all about living in harmony with nature. Imagine taking a deep breath of crisp forest air and feeling a strong sense of togetherness all around you. Sounds cool? At off-grid communities, people have come together to build a super cool living off the grid communities.

They've built homes that are kind to the planet, using stuff like wood and other natural things. You've got cozy cob houses and adorable cabins—each one is like a piece of art, showing how you can live in style while being gentle to Mother Earth. 

But guess what? Earthaven isn't just about cool houses. It's a place where everyone works as a team. They grow their own food, learn from each other, and cheer each other on as they choose a more thoughtful way of life. Earthaven is where nature's melody mixes with the warmth of human connection. If you're looking for off-grid communities where people come together to create a more loving world and live in tune with nature, Earthaven is your dream destination.

Best Off-Grid Comminities #3 Dancing Rabbit Ecovillage

Dancing Rabbit Ecovillage is a super cool village in Missouri, United States surrounded by beautiful hills. It promotes living in a way that's kind to the planet. This place is like a mix of nature's beauty and a strong sense of community. People here want to be friendly to the planet while also making strong bonds with each other. The village is full of off-grid communities that are eco-friendly and gardens that are super lush. The construction project began in late 1980s to make a sustainable eco-village with the help of a team of 65 people. 

What's so awesome about Dancing Rabbit Ecovillage? Well, it's a way of life that's eco-friendly for the world and people. The people who live here make smart choices, like building houses that use less energy and growing food in natural ways. If you're looking for off-grid communities where your love for the planet fits right in and where being green is like a journey everyone takes together, then Dancing Rabbit Ecovillage could be your next preference. 

Best Off-Grid Comminities #4 Emerald Earth

Can you think of a super peaceful living off the grid communities in California, United States called Emerald Earth? It's like a cozy hideout for people who want to feel close to nature. It’s a world where the wind shares secrets with the trees, and everything moves to the rhythm of the Earth's heartbeat. This place isn't like the usual; it's one of the most special off-grid communities where folks live naturally and simply.

Why do people love it? Well, it's like a magnet pulling people who want a simple and mindful life. People come here to enjoy the beauty of keeping things simple and being in tune with nature. This community is sometimes referred to as “Dancing and Drumming community”. They use clean energy from the sun, grow yummy food in their gardens, and share what they know with each other. If you're looking for a peaceful escape from all the craziness, Emerald Earth is like a call to find your connection with nature again. It's a place where you can build a real and respectful relationship with the planet.

Best Off-Grid Comminities #5 Twin Oaks Community

Do you want to experience a peaceful hideaway right in the heart of Virginia, United States? That's the Twin Oaks Community. It's one of the best off-grid communities for nature enthusiasts who want to experience life a bit differently. At Twin Oaks, everyone is equal and shares their responsibilities. It's like a big family where they grow gardens, make things by hand, and enjoy all sorts of crafts.

Here comes the actual question: What's so cool about Twin Oaks Community? It's the chance to truly connect with others and have a purpose that they all share. People live together in a way that feels good, where they help each other out and celebrate together. This community comprises of more than 70 adult members and 15 children. They make stuff with their hands, eating meals as a group, and exploring art—that's what brings them joy. It's like waking up to nature's music and knowing you're part of a crew that cares about what you're good at.

Best Off-Grid Comminities #6 Three Rivers Recreational Area

Do you love adventure and want to enjoy the breathtaking living off the grid communities of Oregon, United States? Well, here comes the Three Rivers Recreational Area. It's a 4000-acre forest community where nature's whispers and flowing rivers are your constant friends, and the simple things in life shine the brightest. This spot is all about getting closer to nature and stepping away from busy city life. With plenty of space for outdoor fun, like hiking and fishing, Three Rivers is one of the most special off-grid communities for all your wild adventures.

What makes Three Rivers so special? It's the idea of being self-sufficient and feeling deeply connected to the great outdoors. Living off the grid, making your own electricity, and finding your own water give you a real sense of power and freedom. People here build their off-grid communities surrounded by tall trees and next to clear rivers, creating a life that respects and honors the land.

●Water: Your Lifeline Off-Grid

When living off the grid communities, having a source of clean water is crucial. Collecting and preserving rainwater or using nearby natural sources ensures you have a steady water supply to depend on. You should always have reserved sources of water just in case there is a scarcity of water in the region where you’re living.

●Food: Sustenance for Off-Grid Adventures

Growing your own food or sourcing it locally becomes vital to off-grid living. Cultivating a garden and learning about edible plants in your area empowers you to enjoy fresh, nutritious meals. It also reduces your reliance on external sources. Learn some basics about gardening and growing your own veggies in your local region.
